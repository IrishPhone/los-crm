We are pleased to announce the dates of International Conference Laser Optics ICLO 2022.

International Conference Laser Optics takes place biennially in Saint-Petersburg, Russia. ICLO is a traditional scientific event in the field of laser physics, optics, and photonics.

The next year ICLO 2022 will be held from 20 to 24 June, 2022. The deadline for paper submission is 13 January, 2022.

ICLO 2022 technical program includes selected topic symposium, as well as plenary, parallel and poster sessions. Distinguished plenary and invited speakers will present state-of-the-art developments in each of the conference topic categories:

  • Solid-State Lasers
  • High Power Lasers: Fiber, Solid State, Gas and Hybrid
  • Semiconductor Lasers, Materials and Applications
  • Laser Beam Control
  • Super-Intense Light Fields and Ultra-Fast Processes
  • Lasers and Systems for Imaging, Green Photonics and Sustainability
  • Free Electron Lasers
  • Nonlinear Photonics: Fundamentals and Applications
  • Optical Nanomaterials
  • Nonlinear and Quantum Integrated Optics

Additionally, the Conference will host the 7th International Symposium on Lasers in Medicine and Biophotonics.

One-page summaries, as submitted by the authors, of all accepted peer-reviewed papers that meet IEEE quality review standards may be eligible for inclusion in the IEEE Xplore Digital Library.

The Conference Program also includes the Exhibition where institutions and companies will have an opportunity to present their solutions related to lasers and photonics. Please contact exhibition@laseroptics.ru for the details and application form, if you would like to participate in the Exhibition.

In 2020 ICLO attracted more than 1200 attendees, and nearly 1000 presentations from 39 countries. The proceedings are available at IEEE Xplore.
